## Task packet

**Linear issue:** UTV2-450 — Expand market alias coverage — add MLB, NHL, NFL spreads/ML/totals to canonical mapping

**Why it matters:**
`results-resolver.ts` only has ~18 player prop aliases registered. MLB, NHL, NFL, and NCAAB/NCAAF game-line markets (spreads, moneylines, totals) don't have canonical alias entries. When a pick uses a canonical key but `game_results` has the SGO raw key with no alias, grading skips that pick permanently with `game_result_not_found`. Expanding coverage means grading works for all active markets.
