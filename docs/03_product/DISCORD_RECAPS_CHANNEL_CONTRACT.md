## Purpose

`discord:recaps` is the aggregated settlement recap surface.

It receives daily and weekly recap posts generated by the RecapAgent after settlement runs complete. Its audience is any member who wants a digest view of recent results — wins, losses, ROI, and CLV context — without monitoring individual pick channels in real time.

---

## Distinct from Per-Pick Settlement Recap

There are two recap-related delivery flows in the system. They are different:

| Flow | Delivery target | Trigger | Content |
|------|-----------------|---------|---------|
| Per-pick settlement recap | Pick's original delivery channel | Each graded pick | Single-pick result embed |
| Aggregated recap | `discord:recaps` | RecapAgent scheduler | Daily/weekly digest embed |

`discord:recaps` only receives the **aggregated** flow. The per-pick flow posts to the channel where the pick was originally delivered (e.g., best-bets or trader-insights). These flows do not overlap.

---

## Access

`discord:recaps` is a **VIP+ or broader** surface.

Recap content is non-sensitive — it is settlement history, not forward-looking intelligence. Broad access is appropriate. The exact role gate is governed by the Discord role access policy, not this contract. This contract governs content and delivery rules only.

---

## What Recaps Is

`discord:recaps` is:
- a scheduled settlement digest channel
- the canonical destination for RecapAgent aggregated posts
- a member-facing performance transparency surface
- a source of proof that the system is working as expected

`discord:recaps` is not:
- a per-pick delivery channel
- a picks feed or promotion channel
- an intelligence or analysis channel
- a testing or debug lane
- a channel for raw data dumps or operator-internal content

---

## Content Rules

### Allowed

- Daily recap embeds: slate results, win/loss/push, flat-bet ROI, CLV% summary
- Weekly recap embeds: extended window, rolled stats
- System-generated recap posts from `RecapAgent`

### Prohibited

- Per-pick delivery embeds (those go to best-bets / trader-insights / etc.)
- Ungraded picks or open positions
- Internal operator data not intended for member visibility
- Debug output or system log excerpts
- Manual capper commentary or human-authored posts (this is an automated channel)

---

## Delivery Rules

Recap posts to this channel are:
- **automated** — generated by `RecapAgent` on a cron schedule
- **idempotent** — duplicate recap runs for the same window do not double-post
- **fail-silent** — if no `DISCORD_BOT_TOKEN` is present or no settled picks exist in the window, no post is made
- **content-gated** — a recap with zero settled picks in the window is suppressed (no empty embeds)

---

## Volume Rule

`discord:recaps` is a low-frequency channel by design.

Expected cadence:
- Daily: 0–1 posts per day (only when there is graded activity to report)
- Weekly: 1 post per week (Friday or Sunday, depending on slate cadence)

If the channel is posting multiple times per day outside of a weekly rollup, investigate the RecapAgent scheduler configuration.

---

## Rollback Rule

If a bad recap post is delivered:
- Operator manually deletes the Discord message
- Root cause (grading data, RecapAgent logic, or embed formatting) is fixed before next scheduled run
- No correcting recap post is issued — deletion is sufficient
- Audit note is written if the bad post reflected incorrect settlement data

---

## Operating Test

Before each recap post:

> "Does this post reflect finalized, graded settlement data for a meaningful window, formatted for member consumption?"

If the data is preliminary, ungraded, or the window is empty — suppress the post.
